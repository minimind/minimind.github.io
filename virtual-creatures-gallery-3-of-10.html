Virtual creatures gallery 3 of 10

Published: Thu 12 August 2010
By Ian Macinnes

In Artificial Intelligence.

This is another post in an occasional series of other peoples' videos of evolved virtual creatures. Lee Graham of Stellar Alchemy has produced great virtual creatures, some of which move beautifully. He keeps a zoo of the best that people have sent in using his software. I especially like the movement of some of these and also the music playing, since normally virtual creature videos are deathly silent. This is my favourite.
